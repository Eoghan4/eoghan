Calendar Buddy

For adding rosters to Google Calendar


Add your roster from an excel file to your Google Calendar with ease!

With an intuitive interface, all you need to do is type in your name, and the Rosters Directory. Calendar Buddy does the rest!

Save time by automating a tedious process with Calendar Buddy!

Manually adding each shift from your roster into Google Calendar can be time-consuming and tedious.

Similarly, opening an Excel file with eveyone's shifts and searching for your own name everytime you want to see your roster for the week is a pain.

With Calendar Buddy, you have the convenience of having your roster saved to Google Calendar, without the stress of adding each shift manually.
