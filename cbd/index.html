About

Calendar Buddy is a tool for uploading work rosters to Google Calendar. If you get your roster in a large Excel file that isn't easy to sift through, Calendar Buddy is for you! Simply upload the file, enter your name and Calendar Buddy does the rest!

The Python-based Desktop version of Calendar Buddy is no longer supported. Instead, check out the web-based version by clicking the link at the top of the page!
